Cosmo Access (CA)
...

Anyone with this title means they're the founder of CSPI, or very trustworthy, and has access to everything in CSPI. Not only that, but they may be a best friend of I2rys in real life.

Board of Access (BOA)
...

BOA users also have access to everything in the CSPI, but on a limited basis; they also manage and make decisions alternatively for CSPI if the CA is busy.

Maintainers
...

Maintainers are responsible for managing user's tasks, including tracking their progress, etc.

Programmers
...

The purpose of programmers is to create services for CSPI that will help it's development. Basically, to create services that meet the vision of CSPI.

Hackers
...

Hackers have a vital role for CSPI this includes finding exploit to websites, assisting the hunters or spies, hackers also have a great mind when it comes to breaking things and being a programmer (Possibly).

Reverse Engineers (Halted)
...

The purpose of reverse engineers is to gather information about a possible malicious file using their own techniques and methods, but since there are already tons of organizations doing this, this role is Halted.

Crawlers
...

The purpose of crawlers is to find specific information such as possible fraudulent Discord users, malicious Discord servers and other things (A bit like spies, but they gather information from the internet.).

Hunters
...

Hunters are like spies, but they're offensive, not defensive, because they're sometimes tasked with hacking or social engineering the bad guys to get vital information.

Spies
...

The purpose of spies is to gather information in order to monitor active bad actors in all services in order to gather more vital information. They're basically a surveillance camera.

Assists
...

Assistants are users that the higher-level roles can get help from when they need it. They're basically like side characters but over all they have universal tasks rather than specific.
